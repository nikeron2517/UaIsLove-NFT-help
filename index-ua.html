The consequences of the fighting in Bucha

People of Eastern Ukraine are left with no home and no one to rely on.Tens of civilian facilities such as living buildings, schools, hospitals are destroyed. People lose their jobs.

In this situation they have to flee home seeking peace for their children in Western parts of the country and in Europe. The Ukrainian government can’t financially compensate expenses and help citizens, therefore, at this moment, all those families can only count on themselves and external help.
